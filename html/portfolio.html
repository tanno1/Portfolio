Engineering
1. Dirtbike Restoration

One of my most recent and involved projects is my dirtbike, a 2011 KX250 F. I purchased this bike in very poor condition at the beginning of 2021. this was my first project with a four stroke engine. The first thing I noticed was for fluid leaking out of the front fork seals. I dove right into the forks and replaced the seals, dust caps, and fork fluid. As a precaution, I also dissasembled the preloader and cleaned it up as well. Next, I replaced the chain, sprokets, and brake system. This project has taught me a lot about maintaining engines, how they operate, and given me hands on experience with diagnosing problems and solving them. One of the next projects I will do is rebuild the top end of the engine.

2. Stand Up Jetski

My first true engineering project began in my sophomore year of high school. I purchased a poorly maintained JS 440 stand up jet ski for $200 on craigslist and fixed it up over the summer. This was a long process and required lots of planning and time. Some of the relevant things I did were: replace all fuel lines, replace all gaskets, add a radius limiting disk for turning that I cut with an oxy fuel torch, and replace the battery, stator and flywheel. I also replaced the electrical box and fully rewired every electrical component. I did a few cosmetic upgrades as well and replaced the grips, side mat and buttons. To this day I still ride the ski in the summer and it functions flawlessly after all the work I did.

3. PC build

1. Pacer Digital

Pacer is a company I have ran for the past four years. Starting from the ground up with the vision of making clothing, my friend Jake Strahm and I started screen printing T shirts in my basement. Through constant hard work we generated over $30,000 of revenue, produced everything from a T shirt to rugs, hats, cargo pants, jeans, and even 3D printed figurines that I designed in Blender. Pacer to this day is a registered business and it has had a great impact on helping me learn how to overcome obstacles to design what I want, how to manage large sums of money, how to pay taxes, and how to organize events for hundreds of people to attend. In the downloadable file there are images of some of the things we have made.
