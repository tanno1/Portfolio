1. Dirtbike Restoration

One of my most recent and involved projects is my dirtbike, a 2011 KX250 F. I purchased this bike in veyr poor condition at the beginning of 2021. this was my first project with a four stroke engine. The first thing I noticed was for fluid leaking out of the front fork seals. I dove right into them and replaced the seals, dust caps, and fork fluid. As a precaution, I also dissasembled the preloader and cleaned it up as well. Next, I replaced the chain, sprokets, and brake system. One of the next projects I will do is rebuild the top end of the engine.
